# ARNAV KUSHWAHA — INTERACTIVE MANGA PORTFOLIO

## Core Vision

This portfolio should NOT look like a traditional developer portfolio.

This portfolio should feel like an interactive digital manga/anime experience where visitors scroll through the story of Arnav Kushwaha's journey as a developer, researcher, and builder.

The goal is to create a memorable experience that recruiters, clients, and collaborators have likely never seen before.

The website should feel like reading a premium manga, motion comic, or anime opening sequence rather than browsing a standard portfolio.
